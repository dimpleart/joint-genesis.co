French Maritime Pine Bark

French Maritime Pine Bark is derived from the bark of pine trees found primarily along the coast of southwest France. Rich in antioxidants, it has been traditionally used to combat inflammation and improve blood circulation, making it beneficial for joint health and overall well-being.

Mobilee

Mobilee is a patented ingredient known for its high concentration of hyaluronic acid, collagen, and other polysaccharides. It plays a crucial role in lubricating the joints, ensuring smooth movement and reducing friction that can lead to wear and tear.

Boswellia Serrata

Boswellia Serrata, often referred to as Indian frankincense, is a herbal extract recognized for its potent anti-inflammatory properties. It has been traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine to treat a variety of conditions, including arthritis and joint pain.

Ginger Root

Ginger Root is a common culinary spice with a rich history in traditional medicine. It possesses anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties, making it effective in alleviating joint pain and discomfort.

BioPerine

BioPerine is an extract derived from black pepper. It's primarily included in supplements to enhance the bioavailability of other ingredients, ensuring that they are absorbed efficiently by the body.

Hyaluronic Acid

Hyaluronic Acid is a naturally occurring substance in the body, primarily found in the skin and joints. In the context of joint health, it acts as a lubricant, reducing friction and ensuring smooth joint movement. Its inclusion in Joint Genesis helps replenish the body's natural levels, promoting joint comfort and flexibility.
